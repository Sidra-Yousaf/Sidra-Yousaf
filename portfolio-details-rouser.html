Portfolio Details

Project Description

I had the pleasure of developing a dynamic and engaging custom WordPress theme for Rouser, a cutting-edge organization focused on climate activism. Rouser's mission is to awaken as many people as possible to the urgent need for action on climate change and ecological collapse through innovative and impactful interventions. I designed and developed the custom WordPress theme using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, WordPress, React, and Advanced Custom Fields (ACF). The theme was carefully crafted to ensure a responsive, pixel-perfect design across all devices, providing an optimal user experience. One of the standout features of the website is the interactive bubble animation. As users scroll through the content, bubbles appear with fade-in and fade-out effects, creating a visually engaging experience. At random intervals, these bubbles burst, adding an element of surprise and delight. This feature was particularly appreciated by the client for its ability to attract users and keep them engaged with the content.
